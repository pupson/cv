Facebook/Meta Unity Real-time AR/VR Work Experience


During my first assignment at Facebook, now Meta, I was assigned as the Technical artist
to the Portal TV (codename "Catalina") AR team creating an online Lip Sync experience.
I initially started making playable prototypes in Unity, using an Xbox connect
to simulate the potential AR capapbilites of the in progress Catalina unit.
This included creating a full naviagable menu system, dressing room with constrained props
and the lip sync experience itself with simulated FX
Once we had design buyoff, I helped direct our vendors in the creation of final assets,
coded the Unity C# game scripts, adapted final assets to make them performant, wrote HLSL shaders
to further increase performance, managed a cross-functional effort with the Facebook Audio team
to integrate their beat detection software to help drive our effects, and then created, animated,
and programmed, the logic for the cameras and most of the FX in the final game.
